Guizhou is located in the southeastern part of Southwest China. It is adjacent to Hunan, Guangxi, Yunnan, Sichuan and Chongqing, and the provincial capital is Guiyang. There are many mountainous terrain in Guizhou Province, which is known as "eight mountains, one water and one field", which is the only province without plain support in the whole country. Guizhou is a beautiful and picturesque landscape, which is one of the most typical areas of karst geomorphology in the world, and has the most colorful Karst landscape.
